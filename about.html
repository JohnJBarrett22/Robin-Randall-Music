Meet your online mentor, Robin

Robin Randall, a longtime songwriter, pianist, and music instructor. She has been a professional songwriter for 45+ years, having had her first song recorded for a CBS TV movie at only 17 years old. She has had many songs recorded by artists & bands and had many songs in television, film, radio and streaming, most notably, the songs she wrote for the 90s iconic TV show, Baywatch. She has been a music instructor for kids, teens and adults for 35+ years and loves teaching, inspiring, encouraging and supporting her students.

Robin's Current Philosophy

For many years Robin taught private music lessons out of her home. That seemed to go well until Covid hit. All the sudden life stopped. Social distancing and staying 6 feet apart didn't work with instructing piano or songwriting students in her living room workspace. The students abruptly dropped off in March of 2020. So, Robin reinvented herself in light of the new world we live in. She discovered a working method to teach online through Zoom where she could reach above and beyond her living room and into the entire USA and world. She has now had students from across the the United States, China, Brazil, Sweden, Germany, UK, Australia and more.
