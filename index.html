Platinum and gold selling songwriter

Robin has had success in songwriting since she was 17 years old with her first placed song in a CBS made for TV movie that was rated #1 that week. Since then she has had continued success in television, film, radio and streaming.

Accomplished live and recorded pianist

Robin graduated from the Dick Grove School of Music with high honors in Keyboard Instruction and Songwriting. She has used her skills both in live performances, on tour and playing gigs, and in recording sessions for artists and television.

Highly sought after music theory instructor

After 8 levels of Harmony & Theory classes, Robin became the highest achieving student in her program and received an award at Dick Grove School of Music for her abilities in this area. She has aptly passed on this knowledge to thousands of songwriting and piano students.

Years of teaching at L.A.'s best music schools

Many years as a prominent instructor at Musician's Institute, Songwriting School Of Los Angeles, Los Angeles County High School for the Arts (LACHSA), Dick Grove's School of Music, the Los Angeles Recording Workshop and the Learning Annex.
